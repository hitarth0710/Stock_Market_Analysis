Interpretation:

The decomposition breaks down the time series into three components:

  • Trend: The long-term progression of the series (increasing, decreasing, or stable).
  • Seasonality: The repeating patterns or cycles over fixed periods.
  • Residual: The random variation in the series that cannot be attributed to trend or seasonality.

Interpretation:

ACF and PACF plots help identify potential ARIMA parameters:

  • ACF (Autocorrelation Function): Shows correlation between a time series and its lagged values. Helps identify the MA(q) component.
  • PACF (Partial Autocorrelation Function): Shows correlation between a time series and its lagged values, while controlling for the values of the time series at all shorter lags. Helps identify the AR(p) component.
